H2 0-0S(0) 12.3 µm (Z = 1) line intensity

pdrtpy ModelSet: wk2006

H2 0-0S(0) 12.3 µm (Z = 1) line intensity emitted from the surface of a PDR as a function of the cloud density \(n\) and the FUV flux incident on the cloud \(G_0\) .   Contours are labeled with the line intensity in units of erg cm-2 s-1 sr-1.   Models of Kaufman et al. 2006 are used. The FITS file is available for download.
